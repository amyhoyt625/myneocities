M y T r a v e l s

I love to travel and this past year I have fallen in love with solo travelling. The summer prior to my trip, I studied in Greece, traveling throughout the country with my peers and professors. I think that trip fuled my love for traveling even furthur and also inspired me to be on the move while travelling. Thus my 12 countries in 2 months euro trip. I stayed in hostels and met so many new people, made new friends and experienced and saw so many new things.I took the eurail for the majority of my trip and also took a few flix buses and ryan air flights along the way. I really want to got to Brazil and Peru.

I really enjoyed taking pictures during my travels on my ancient cheap ebay digital camera. I also have a building travel journal that I hope to fill with stories from every continent!.
